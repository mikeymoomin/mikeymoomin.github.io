My Story

Hello! I'm a Chartered Accountant based in the UK who is diving deep into the world of computer science. After several years in finance, I discovered my passion for programming and technology, which led me to begin this learning journey.

This blog serves as my digital garden where I document my learnings, challenges, and breakthroughs as I explore various aspects of computer science, from programming languages to algorithms, data structures, and machine learning.

My background in accounting gives me a unique perspective on problem-solving and data analysis, which I find incredibly valuable as I transition into more technical domains.

What I'm Learning

My current focus areas include:

  • Full-stack web development
  • Python programming and data analysis
  • Algorithms and data structures
  • Machine learning fundamentals
  • Software engineering best practices

I believe in learning by doing, which is why I try to build practical projects alongside my theoretical studies. You can check out my Projects page to see what I've been working on.
